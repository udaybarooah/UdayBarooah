Agentic Workflows & Dashboards

A selection of systems I have designed and built end to end, from data pipeline to interface. These power real operations in professional sport, e-commerce and marketing.

Every example here is deidentified. Client and club names, athlete data and any commercially sensitive figures have been removed or replaced with representative descriptions. Live walkthroughs are available on request.

Professional sport

Athlete monitoring and live game dashboards

A suite of dashboards for an elite rugby league program. A live in-game tool tracks ball-in-play, interchange management and player minutes from real-time feeds, while a GPS and athlete-demand dashboard processes Catapult data into session loads, training-quality scores and individual deep-dives. Pre and post-game reports are generated automatically as shareable pages for performance, medical and coaching staff.

Built with  R, Shiny, SQLite, Catapult and live tracking APIs, automated HTML report generation

E-commerce operations

Live Ops platform with agentic alerting

An operational monitoring platform for a direct-to-consumer eyewear brand. It unifies Shopify and third-party fulfilment data into a single Live Ops view, and a weekly review surface generates a Monday-morning narrative briefing. An agentic layer watches order, fulfilment and stock signals and posts plain-language alerts to Slack so the team catches issues before they cascade.

Built with  Next.js, FastAPI, Postgres and Supabase, Shopify and 3PL fulfilment integrations, Claude for narrative generation, Railway

Marketing, Subject:RAW

Growth OS demand-report engine

A white-label engine that turns a clinic's website, location and segment into a credible one-page market report showing the local patient demand they are missing. It runs an intake to data-pull to demand-calculation to assembly pipeline, drawing on search, places and ad-library data, and exports a branded report as both web page and PDF. Includes an SEO and visibility audit build for the same clients.

Built with  Python, FastAPI, Postgres, Jinja and HTMX, third-party data APIs, Claude, Railway

Marketing, Subject:RAW

Automated creative and graphics workflow

A custom application that automates repeatable design and graphics production for an agency. It standardises brand output across clients and removes manual steps from the creative pipeline, letting a small team produce a far larger volume of consistent, on-brand work.

Built with  Custom app, automated design pipeline, brand-token system

Finance and retail

Sales-deck generator

A tool that converts raw market notes into a real, editable PowerPoint deck. It extracts a brand kit from a template, builds native editable charts rather than flat images, and supports slide-by-slide refinement through plain-English feedback, with live preview and PDF export.

Built with  Python, FastAPI, python-pptx, LibreOffice headless, Claude, Railway
